Payment Procedure

  1. Go to your M-Pesa menu.
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa.
  3. Choose PayBill.
  4. Enter Business Number: 9824375.
  5. Enter Account Number: Your Registered Phone Number.
  6. Enter the Amount (Loan + Service Fee).
  7. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and press OK.
